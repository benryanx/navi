Navi
AI screen guide — shows you exactly where to click
1
Install Ollama
Navi uses Ollama to run AI models locally on your machine. Free and private — nothing leaves your PC.
2
Download the AI model
Run this once in your terminal — downloads Gemma 4 (~9.6 GB). Only needed once.
ollama pull gemma4
3
You're ready!
Press Ctrl+F to speak and ask Navi anything about your screen.
