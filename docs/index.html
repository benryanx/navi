How it works

Three seconds to an answer

🎙

1. Ask out loud

Press Ctrl+F and speak your question — "How do I add a filter in DaVinci Resolve?"

👁

2. Navi sees your screen

Navi captures your screen and sends it to a local AI model. Nothing ever leaves your machine.

✦

3. Buddy flies to the answer

The Navi buddy flies to the exact UI element, labels it "Here!" and explains what to do next.

Features

Built different

🔒

100% local & private

Everything runs on your machine via Ollama. Your screen, your voice, your questions — none of it ever leaves your PC.

🎯

Points to the exact pixel

Not just text — Navi visually flies to the button, menu, or field you need to click. No more hunting around.

🗣

Voice-first

Hold Ctrl+F and speak naturally. Whisper transcribes locally, so no internet needed for voice input either.

✨

ElevenLabs voice support

Use a natural AI voice for responses. Plug in your ElevenLabs API key in settings and pick any voice.

⚡

Works in any app

Blender, Photoshop, DaVinci Resolve, Outlook — if it's on your screen, Navi can guide you through it.

🪟

Always-on overlay

Navi lives as a transparent overlay. It follows your mouse, stays out of the way, and never steals focus.
